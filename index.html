Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients: Chicken, butter, cream, tomatoes, spices.

Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular vegetarian dish consisting of marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

Ingredients: Paneer, yogurt, spices, bell peppers.

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a traditional North Indian dish made with spicy chickpeas served with deep-fried bread.

Ingredients: Chickpeas, spices, flour, yogurt.

Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy lentil dish made with black lentils and kidney beans.

Ingredients: Black lentils, kidney beans, cream, spices.

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian dish made with spinach and paneer in a creamy sauce.

Ingredients: Spinach, paneer, spices, cream.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with spices, meat, and vegetables.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat, spices, saffron.
